Left: Some things I like Below: My story

I've always loved video games, especially Nintendo ones. I particularly enjoy the characters and there is just a certain charm and likability to the characters of the games that I play.

So one day, I picked up a pencil and sketchbook, and started drawing my inkling from Splatoon 2. I did it because I was inspired by the many Splatoon artists on Twitter who have and draw their own Splatoon OCs (Original creations/Original characters). My drawing wasn't the best, but it was a start to an amazing journey down the artistic path.

Drawing, one of my many hobbies, is fun and theraputic, and it allows me to express myself through the things I draw. While the characters I draw are not necessarily of my own creation (as they are characters from video games), but I can add my own twist and make my drawings unique and personal.
